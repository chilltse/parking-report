# **Conflict Resolution Protocol:**


## 1. **Weekly Merge Review** – every Sunday the team meets to review pending pull requests, agree on merge order, and resolve any conflicts.
## 2. **Merge Gatekeeper** – only Nanxuan Xie has permission to perform the final code merge once all conflicts are settled.
## 3. **Issue Tracking & Escalation** – any conflicts not resolved during the Sunday review are documented in our issue tracker; if still unresolved by mid-week, they’re escalated to the highest priority issue after a second review meeting.
